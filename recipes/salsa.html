Roasted Tomato Salsa

Description

Roasted tomatoes, peppers and aromatics make a flavorful, delicious salsa.

Ingredients

  • 4 Roma tomatoes
  • 1 Red onion
  • 2 Jalapenos (seeds and ribs removed, if desired)
  • Juice of half a lime
  • 1 Bunch of cilantro
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven broil on high.
  2. Cut tomatoes and onion into quarters. Slice jalapenos in half and remove seeds, if desired.
  3. Place vegetables onto a cookie sheet and spray with olive oil.
  4. Broil vegetables until slightly charred, about 5-10 minutes.
  5. Remove tray from oven and add all ingredients into a blender. Season to taste.
  6. Blend until almost smooth and serve with tortilla chips or your favorite Mexican dishes.
